2023 Aveiro

(DETAILS)

MATERIALS
Munken Pure 90gr
Ultramarine Canson Iris Vivaldi coloured paper 185gr

COLLABORATORS

(INFO)

2024 Virgem que Pariu

(DETAILS)

MATERIALS
Fluorescent Pink Clairefontaine Trophée coloured paper 80gr
Fuchsia Canson Iris Vivaldi coloured paper 185gr

SIZE
112×223mm

COLLABORATORS
Cláudia Barros
Marta Cabral
Sofia Gouveia
      

(INFO)

Virgem que pariu seeks to invoke the Madonna Whore complex. An object of study for psychoanalysis, it explores the duality between erotic women (whores, sexually liberated) and chaste women (saints and sexually repressed). In this sense, this publication sets out to associate and oppose antagonistic concepts, deconstructing them. It is made up of paintings by men, fonts drawn by women and definitions from the priberam dictionary.

(INFO)

The graphic identity of the "Agora EU 2024" event is a vibrant and dynamic visual system that reflects the energy and collaborative spirit of youth programs. 2024 Tá Fechado

(DETAILS)

MATERIALS
varied supports

SIZE
varied dimensions

COLLABORATORS
Íris Silva
Marta Cabral
Ricardo Sousa
Rita Soares
Rui Marques
Sofia Gouveia
      

(INFO)

This project was developed for the 2024 Finalists Exhibition for the Communication Design Degree at FBAUP. The exhibition celebrated the end of a cycle and its “archiving”. Having “the process” as the main theme we wanted to highlight a part of the work that is sometimes overlooked, but which is often the most important, as it reveals the true essence of the project. This year's distinguishing feature is the inclusion of the digital environment. These elements were chosen because this class was the first to enter the design world during the Covid-19 pandemic, and their first contact with university life was through a screen.

2023 AlfaBelas

(DETAILS)

MATERIALS
Munken Lynx 90gr
Munken Lynx 240g

SIZE
300×405mm

COLLABORATORS
Marta Cabral
      

(INFO)

The aim of this publication is to compose a Roman alphabet using images of spaces at the Faculty of Fine Arts in Porto which, at first glance, bear no resemblance to the Roman alphabet, but which, with a certain framing and an attentive eye, metamorphose into these characters. In this way, an unusual and surprising alphabet is constructed. Analog photography proved to be the most appropriate medium for the project, giving it a certain timelessness. To give this work a greater purpose, we chose to associate it with the celebration of the institution's 250th anniversary.

2022 ao olhares para cima lembra-te dos pássaros postiços

(DETAILS)

MATERIALS
glass sheet 10 mm
printing paper

SIZE
150×200mm

(INFO)

Interested in the contrast between the artificial and the natural, I recalled the stories "The Nightingale" (1843), by Hans Christian Andersen, and the Greek myth of Icarus. In both stories, human ambition and greed are highlighted, contrasted with the raw purity of nature. In both cases, the “artificial” wings, which at first seem to be a sign of freedom, turn out to be a curse.
